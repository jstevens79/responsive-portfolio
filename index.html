About Me

I was born in middle-of-nowhere Indiana, surrounded by cows and corn. Bored with the prospects of farm life, I focused my energies on art and music.

I started off my adult life pursuing a career in youth ministry but found myself spending all my free time doing graphic design. Eventually, I made it my full-time focus and took a job as a web designer, learning how to code HTML and CSS on the job.

Through the years, I've become more focused on development, and am looking forward to understanding the backend as well as I do the front so that I can bring my creative ideas to life.
